Connecting the Heart of the Andes

🦙 The Yupaychay Association engages in projects for the emancipation of Quechua communities by sharing their work, language, culture, and promoting the exchange of knowledge between the Andes and other cultures.

Our Mission

Preserving a Rich Heritage 🌎

Founded on the principles of community, respect, and stewardship, our association works to empower Quechua-speaking communities. We believe that by fostering a strong network, we can ensure the vibrancy and continuity of our ancestral knowledge and traditions in the modern world.

💬 Language Preservation

Promoting the use and study of the Quechua language through educational programs and resources.

🧶 Cultural Exchange

Organizing events and workshops to share the beauty of Quechua garments, arts, music, and traditions.

🫂 Community Empowerment

Supporting local initiatives that enhance economic opportunities and social well-being.
